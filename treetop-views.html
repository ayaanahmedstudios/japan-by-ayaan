Treetop Views.
1/100 f/5 Sumida, Japan 16-12-2022

The tallest building in Japan is also called the Tokyo Skytree, and I just happened to find my way there at the most optimal time, I was running late from being at the Shibuya Skydeck, and somehow reached an hour past my ticket booking, which led to me to enter exactly at sunset.

There was a flock of photographers pushing against each other at the side of the tower facing Mount Fuji and also coincidentally also facing the sunset. I just barely managed to grab a few photographs of that, and instead, photographed this view, on the other side, of purely the metropolitan city Tokyo really is, a river running through it, sunset in the sky, and an oddly shaped building in the middle-right, which I later researched to find was the Edo-Tokyo museum and loosely based on traditional concepts of Japanese architecture.
